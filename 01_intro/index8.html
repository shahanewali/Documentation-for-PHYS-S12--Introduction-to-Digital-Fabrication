<br> #### Week 8: CNC Milling, Molding & Casting <i> Assignment: Design something and make it using CNC. You may use the ShopBot (2D outlines), the Shaper (2D outlines) or the SRM-20 (2.5D molds). Create something by molding and casting.</i> And here is a <a href="https://nathanmelenbrink.github.io/intro-dig-fab/07_cnc/index.html">Link to the original assignment page</a> <p>For this week's assignment, I didn't feel like using the shopbot at all. I'm already scared enough of saws, drills and so on.</p> <p>I decided to do two assignments in one by milling a mold first and using the SRM-20 Mill.</p> <p>I decided to make a star pyramid because why not?</p> <p>The star fits into a 45 mm diameter circle and is almost 20mm high. To make my "pyramid", I used the offset function to create lots of stars from the large star. I then extruded them at different levels and ended up using the fillet function to make my star pyramid even prettier. My mold is a square, 62mm on the outside and 50mm on the inside. The walls are 6mm thick.</p> <img src="etoilepyramide1.png" alt="Image" style="width:400px;height:231px;"/> <img src="etoilepyramide2.png" alt="Image" style="width:400px;height:328px;"/> <img src="etoilepyramide3.png" alt="Image" style="width:400px;height:347px;"/>
